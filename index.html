Our Mission

Pushpak Society is a vibrant community of innovators and creators at RKGIT. We are dedicated to fostering creativity, innovation, and collaboration among students. Through workshops, hackathons, and projects, we empower students to turn their ideas into reality.

Our goal is to create a platform where students can learn new technologies, share their knowledge, and build amazing projects together.
